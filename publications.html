AfriVox: Probing Multilingual and Accent Robustness of Speech LLMs
Busayo Awobade, Mardhiyah Sanni, Tassallah Abdullahi, Chibuzor Okocha, Kelechi Ezema, Devendra Kayande, Lukman Enegi Ismaila, Gloria Ashiya Katuka, Tobi Olatunji
UNDER REVIEW

The NaijaVoices Dataset: Cultivating Large-Scale, High-Quality, Culturally-Rich Speech Data for African Languages
Chris Emezue, NaijaVoices Community, Busayo Awobade, Abraham Owodunni, Handel Emezue, Gloria Emezue, Nefertiti Emezue, Sewade Ogun, Bunmi Akinremi, David Adelani, Chris Pal
Interspeech 2025
Creation and validation of NaijaVoices, a novel 1,800-hour speech dataset for low-resource Nigerian languages (Igbo, Hausa, and Yoruba), addressing critical data scarcity and enabling substantial performance gains in automatic speech recognition models.

From Scarcity to Efficiency: Investigating the Effects of Data Augmentation on African Machine Translation
Busayo Awobade*, Mardiyyah Oduwole*, Steven Kolawole*
arXiv
Investigates how data augmentation techniques can enhance machine translation for low-resource African languages. Using sentence concatenation with back translation and switch-out across six languages, the approach demonstrates strong potential for advancing translation in under-resourced settings.
Code
What Happens When Small Is Made Smaller? Exploring the Impact of Compression on Small Data Pretrained Language Models
Busayo Awobade*, Mardiyyah Oduwole*, Steven Kolawole*
AfricaNLP 2024
Demonstrates that compression techniques significantly improve the efficiency and effectiveness of small, low-resource language models, confirming that their benefits are not limited to large-scale models.
